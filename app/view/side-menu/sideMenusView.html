itSideMenus

A container element for side menu(s) and the main content. Allows the left and/or right side menu to be toggled by dragging the main content area side to side. To use side menus, add an it-side-menus parent element. This will encompass all pages that have a side menu, and have at least 2 child elements: 1 it-side-menu-content for the center content, and it-side-menu directives
